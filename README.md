# SBI DigiPilot

### 🚀 A Multi-Agent AI Platform for Intelligent Digital Banking Adoption

> **Empowering Every Customer to Bank Digitally with Autonomous AI Agents.**

## 📌 Overview

SBI DigiPilot is an Agentic AI-powered platform designed to accelerate digital banking adoption by providing customers with a personalized, intelligent banking companion. Unlike traditional chatbots, DigiPilot uses multiple AI agents that collaborate autonomously to guide users through onboarding, digital transactions, fraud prevention, financial education, and personalized banking services.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many customers struggle to adopt digital banking due to:
- Low digital literacy
- Complex onboarding processes
- Fear of online fraud
- Limited awareness of digital banking features
- Dependence on branch visits and customer support

---

## 💡 Solution

SBI DigiPilot leverages **Agentic AI** and **Multi-Agent Systems** to provide proactive, context-aware assistance throughout the customer's digital banking journey.

The platform helps users:
- Register for digital banking
- Activate UPI and Internet Banking
- Perform secure transactions
- Receive fraud alerts
- Learn digital banking services
- Get personalized financial recommendations

# 🧠 AI Agents

## 🟦 Onboarding Agent
Guides customers through account setup, KYC, SBI YONO registration, and UPI activation.

## 🟩 Learning Agent
Explains digital banking services with interactive guidance.

## 🟨 Transaction Agent
Assists with fund transfers, bill payments, UPI, QR payments, and account services.

## 🟥 Fraud Protection Agent
Detects suspicious activities and alerts customers in real time.

## 🟪 Financial Wellness Agent
Analyzes spending and recommends savings, investments, and SBI products.

## 🟧 Customer Support Agent
Answers banking queries and resolves customer issues.
